# BRAND_BRIEF.md

## Brand
AgentFlow by Gen I Labs

## Positioning
Gen I Labs builds aesthetic authority plus operational systems.
AgentFlow is the real-estate-first operating system that helps agents follow up faster, manage leads more cleanly, improve listing copy, and run a more premium business.

## Audience
- Real estate agents
- Small real estate teams
- Property professionals who already get inquiries but lose deals to slow follow-up, messy systems, or weak operational consistency

## Visual identity
- Dark emerald and black cinematic gradient
- Single accent emerald mint
- Optional small orange accent for emphasis
- High contrast
- Premium SaaS / luxury tech-ad realism
- Operator dashboard energy
- Clean compositions with lots of breathing room
- Minimal clutter
- Subtle grain, vignette, and glow where tasteful

## Typography direction
- Strong all-caps condensed hero headlines
- Short supporting lines
- No long dense paragraphs
- Premium editorial spacing

## Tone
- Calm operator energy
- Premium
- Clear
- Systems-first
- Confident, not hypey
- Sharp where needed, but not rude

## Emotional goal
The page should make the visitor feel:
1. “These people understand real estate chaos.”
2. “This looks premium and trustworthy.”
3. “This feels simple enough to start.”
4. “I should book the call.”
